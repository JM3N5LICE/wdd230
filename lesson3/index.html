Lonely Mountain Chamber of Commerce

Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide information through its sub pages including:

  • Home - basic info, community info, weather, calls to action, and business spotlights
  • Discover - historical info, and current demographics, images, relevant local events and info
  • Directory - local businesses in the area
  • Join - membership benefit information, application form adhering to course requirements
  • Contact - essential and extended contact info for chamber officers and board of directees, contact us form requesting name, phone, email, and message area

Each of these pages will feature appropriate titles, logos, headers, footers, navigation, different screen size accomodations, links, colors, and images that create a positive user experience.
