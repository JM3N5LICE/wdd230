Coastal Area

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California. Carlsbad's coastal area is known for its beautiful beaches, including Carlsbad State Beach, which features a long stretch of sandy shorelines, beach volleyball courts, and a seaside boardwalk. Other popular beaches in the area include South Carlsbad State Beach and Terramar Beach.

The coastal area of Carlsbad is also home to several popular spots for birdwatching, hiking, and kayaking. In addition to its natural beauty, Carlsbad's coastal area offers a range of recreational activities, including surfing, fishing, and boating. The area is also known for its world-class golf courses, including the Aviara Golf Club and the La Costa Resort and Spa.
