Colors

Amazon

amazon.com

I decided that the color pallette of amazon is a good example of complementary colors on the color wheel with the dark navy and the various shades of yellow/orange. It is not monochromatic or bland but pops in a way that is appealing and simple. It has also become a memorable color scheme that is associated with Amazon and its branding.

Visual Balance

Under Armour

underarmour.com

For Visual Balance I want to point your attention to the centralized font and the logo on the navigation bar that is centralized and bolder. The symmetry on both sides adds a nice effect and even the image they are using to advertise their sale is visually balanced with the red in the middle and the black on the sides.

Repition

Hoka

hoka.com

For Repition I knew that most sites would have a drop down navigation menu for their site and chose Hoka to fit the theme of athlestic clothes and online shopping. The buttons on the menu are all equally sized and are very similar. This was one of the few repetition options that I could find that would fit on a phone screen to demonstrate the repetitive and clean effect.
